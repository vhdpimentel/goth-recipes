Black Lemonade

Description

Refreshing and spooky, this lemonade looks like some evil magic potion, but tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 3 lemons
  • Ice
  • 4 glasses of water
  • Sugar
  • 2-4 capsules of activated charcoal

Steps

  1. Squeeze the juice out of 2 lemons on a jar
  2. Mix in the charcoal
  3. Add the water
  4. Add sugar to taste
  5. Add some cubes of ice
  6. Serve and add slices of the remaining lemon to the glasses
