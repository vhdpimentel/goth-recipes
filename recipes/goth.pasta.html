Goth Pasta

Description

Simple but delicious, this is a classic.

  • 250g of black pasta (usually made with squid ink, but there's also black bean spaghetti)
  • 6 tomatoes
  • 1 onion
  • A couple tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 250g of seafood of choice
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the tomatoes, onion, garlic and bell pepper however you wish
  2. On a hot pan with a couple tablespoons of olive oil, stir fry:
    • the seafood
    • the onion
    • the bell pepper
    • the garlic
  3. Add the tomatoes and leave it simmering
  4. Bring 3 liters of water to a boil
  5. Add the salt and the pasta to the boiling water
  6. Drain the pasta
  7. Mix into the sauce
  8. Serve while hot
