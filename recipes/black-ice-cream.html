Black Ice Cream

Description

This recipe is for those who have ice-cold blood running in their veins and a sweet tooth as well: the blackest, creamiest, black ice cream you could ever taste. Made with cream, sugar and pure, eternal darkness.

Ingredients

  • 120 ml activated carbon
  • 50 ml vanilla extract
  • 500 ml whole milk
  • 1 liter heavy cream

Steps

  1. Mix the milk, sugar and cream in a pots
  2. Heat up until it's simmering
  3. When it becomes nice and smooth, take away from the fire and mix in the rest of the ingredients
  4. Cover and throw it in the fridge until it has cooled down
  5. Get your ice cream maker ready and pour the mixture in
  6. Put it in whatever container you like and keep it in a freezer, of course
